## Where They Hang Out

- Hacker News (Show HN, Ask HN threads about AI tooling)
- r/ExperiencedDevs, r/cscareerquestions, r/startups
- Indie Hackers community forums and Slack
- Latent Space Discord
- X/Twitter lists: "AI engineers", "YC founders"

## Getting First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 budget)

1. **Show HN post** — "Show HN: I built a free AI spend auditor — enter your tools, get exact savings"
2. **Cold DM 50 YC W24/S24 founders on X** — reference their public job listings that show they hire AI-heavy teams
3. **Post audit results as tweet threads** — "We audited our $800/mo AI stack and found $340 in savings. Here's the breakdown: [shareable URL]"
4. **Latent Space and Lenny's Slack communities** — genuinely useful tool, not spam
5. **Credex existing network** — Credex already sells to these buyers; embed the audit tool in their outreach
