# ECONOMICS.md

> Numbers below are estimates based on public information about Credex's business model. To be refined with real data by Day 6.

## What's a Converted Lead Worth?

Assumption: Credex sells discounted AI credits with ~20% margin, average deal = $5,000/year in credit purchases.
- LTV of one customer: $5,000 × 2.5 years avg retention = **$12,500**
- With 30% gross margin: **$3,750 gross profit per customer**

## CAC at Each Channel

| Channel | Est. CAC | Basis |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Show HN | ~$0 | Organic; time cost only |
| Cold DM (X) | ~$10 | 2 hrs labor / 12 conversions per 50 DMs |
| Credex warm outreach | ~$50 | Sales rep time amortised |
| Paid (future) | ~$200-400 | SaaS B2B paid benchmark |

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Basis |
|-------|------|-------|
| Landing → Audit started | 40% | Typical SaaS landing conversion |
| Audit started → Completed | 70% | Low friction, no login |
| Completed → Email captured | 35% | Email shown after value, not before |
| Email → Consultation booked | 15% | Only high-savings cases shown CTA |
| Consultation → Purchase | 40% | Warm intent signal from audit |

**Blended: 1,000 visitors → 400 audits → 280 completed → 98 emails → 15 consultations → 6 purchases**

## $1M ARR in 18 Months

- Average deal: $5,000/year → need **200 customers**
- At 6 purchases per 1,000 visitors: need **33,000 visitors/month** by month 18
- Achievable via: content SEO + Show HN compounding + referral loop from shareable URLs
- Key assumption: shareable audit URLs drive 30% of traffic via organic sharing

## Math Check

Month 18: 33k visitors × 0.4 × 0.7 × 0.35 × 0.15 × 0.4 = ~196 new customers/mo
Cumulative over 18 months (ramp): ~200 total customers × $5k ARR = **$1M ARR** ✓
